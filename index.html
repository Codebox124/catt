Our Mission

To produce exceptional Maine Coons that are as healthy and loving as they are beautiful.

‚óÜ Trinkets Maine Coons ‚óÜ

All Colors. All Heart. All Maine Coon.

Trinkets is our flagship cattery, where we proudly work with all recognized Maine Coon colors‚Äîincluding classic tabbies, silvers, smokes, torties, solids, and beyond.

We value diversity in both appearance and personality, producing kittens that exemplify the breed standard in size, coat, structure, and temperament. Trinkets kittens are raised in a warm, home-based environment with early socialization, ensuring they grow into confident, affectionate companions.

Whether you seek a show-quality cat or a loyal family member, Trinkets offers a rainbow of possibilities wrapped in gentle, giant hearts.

Rare Elegance. Timeless Warmth. The Phoenix of Maine Coons.

House of Rubies is a boutique cattery specializing exclusively in the rare cinnamon blue color spectrum‚Äîalso known as the phoenix shades.

These uncommon, warm-toned coats with blue undercoats are paired with the same hallmark features of the Maine Coon: luxurious fur, powerful bone structure, and an intelligent, dog-like temperament.

Our Breeding Philosophy

At both Trinkets and House of Rubies, our philosophy is rooted in health, temperament, and genetic integrity.

We believe that a beautiful cat begins with a healthy foundation. All of our breeding cats are rigorously health-tested for HCM, SMA, PK-def, and other breed-specific conditions, and are raised in a stress-free, enriched environment.

We breed with intention‚Äînot just for looks, but for longevity, intelligence, and affectionate nature. Every kitten is nurtured as part of our family until they are ready to join yours.
