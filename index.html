About Our Cattery

Our Story

The Royal Kingdom of Maine Coons was established with a passion for preserving the majestic beauty and gentle nature of the Maine Coon breed. Our journey began over 15 years ago when we fell in love with these magnificent cats and decided to dedicate our lives to breeding healthy, well-socialized Maine Coons.

Our Mission

We are committed to breeding Maine Coons that exemplify the breed standard while maintaining excellent health, temperament, and socialization. Every kitten born in our cattery is raised with love, care, and attention to ensure they become wonderful family companions.

Health Testing

All our breeding cats undergo comprehensive health screening including HCM, PKD, and SMA testing. We believe in transparency and provide health certificates for all our kittens to ensure you're welcoming a healthy addition to your family.

Socialization

Our kittens are raised underfoot in our home environment, ensuring they are well-socialized with children, other cats, and household activities. This early socialization creates confident, well-adjusted cats that adapt easily to their new homes.
